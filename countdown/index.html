About

Activity Timer — a minimal, device‑agnostic timer designed for workshops, workouts, and focus sprints. Scroll the numbers to set a time, use presets for one‑taps, and customize colours and sounds. Sync your settings and timer state with a simple ID across your devices.
Instructions
1) Set time by scrolling H/M/S or tap a preset. 2) Press Start. 3) Pause/Resume as needed. 4) When time’s up, alarm + optional vibration and flashing; timer counts up. Use Restart/Dismiss. Open Settings to tweak appearance, sounds, and sync.
